Key Rules
Warm-up
Always build up gradually: 50% → 65% → 80% → working weight.
Week 12 — Peak
Hit the planned weight first. If the single feels easy — take another attempt with +5–10 kg. Don't risk it earlier in the cycle.
After the Cycle — Deload
1–3 weeks at 50–60% of working weights. Then start a new cycle with your new 1RM as the baseline.
Accessories
Keep accessory weights moderate throughout the cycle. They support the main lifts — don't let them generate extra fatigue.
Progression
+7.5 kg/week on squat and deadlift, +5 kg/week on bench. Stick to the plan even when you feel strong.
